Add custom banners to your site with a simple shortcode generator

== Description ==

House Ads let you insert custom ads to your website by pasting the generated shortcode to your post or page

Example: [house-ads keyword="Keyword" size="leaderboard" url="https://www.wordpress.org" target="_blank" rel=""]

== Installation ==

Upload the House Ads plugin to your blog and Activate it

1, 2, 3: You're done!


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

2. Go to 'Houe Ads' section
3. Insert your desired ad size
4. Publish your Ads group
5. Generate the shortcode with your custom settings for the group including the keywords
6. Copy and paste your shortcode on the desired post or page
7. See it live!
